Many great features

  • A beautiful UI that perfectly fits your phone or tablet device
  • Includes all pharmacies in Cyprus, with geotags
  • Show on-call pharmacies (available after normal business hours)
  • Click on a pharmacy's address to view it on a map
  • Click on a pharmacy's phone number to dial
  • Search for nearby pharmacies
  • Easily share specific pharmacies via email, SMS, etc.
  • Full support for Greek and English (and French and German for that matter)
  • Integrates search functionality (see screenshots)

Privacy policy

Cyprus Pharmacy Guide takes your privacy very seriously!

The only permissions required by the Cyprus Pharmacy Guide app is internet access, which is needed simply to fetch updates regarding pharmacies, and location, which is needed to show you nearby pharmacies. Please note that approximate and anonymous information regarding the device's location at the time of nearby searches might be logged for research purposes.

We also use Google Analytics to trace app usage, and AdMob to serve ads.
